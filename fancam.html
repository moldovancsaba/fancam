Consent, privacy & data governance

Iteration Checklist (Next Workshop)

Work the list top-down in the room: capture decisions, assign owners and dates, then park non-blocking ideas. Duplicate bullets into your issue tracker with step numbers (0–9, 4a–c) in the title.

Decisions & scope (blockers)

  • Contact capture before vs after branded result preview — legal, conversion, and sponsor narrative implications.
  • Single auth path vs maintaining email + social in parallel; deprecation plan if one wins.
  • Which consent combinations are mandatory per region; this demo uses required Privacy + Terms and optional Marketing — confirm 4a–c map to your live checkboxes.
  • Android / in-app browser rules: when to force full browser, popup login, or custom tabs; exact user-agent and WebView detection policy.
  • White-label: mandatory fields (address, company, VAT) vs optional; who validates and stores them.
  • Sponsor vs event CTA precedence when both appear on the same screen (3, 5, 6, 7).

Product & UX

  • Step 2: retake limits, gallery-only events, and messaging when camera permission is denied.
  • Step 3: copy and layout for optional sponsor tile; “continue” vs “skip” semantics.
  • Step 4 gate: inline error copy when login is tapped early; loading states during OAuth return.
  • Step 5: primary action hierarchy — mailbox vs share vs save image.
  • Steps 7–9: empty states, expired events, and takedown UX for public URLs.
  • Accessibility pass on consent checkboxes, policy links, and focus order after tab return.

Legal & consent

  • Policy hosting URLs, version numbering, and what “accept” means for each checkbox.
  • Marketing opt-in vs transactional email — separate proof points in the audit log.
  • Minors / parental flows if relevant to your verticals.
  • Data retention defaults per asset type (raw photo, composite, metadata, consents).
  • Sponsor access to PII: contract checklist and technical enforcement (no leakage in Step 7 analytics).

Commercial & go-to-market

  • Sponsor package definitions tied to UI placements (email, share page, capture interstitial).
  • Reporting package for partners: which KPIs from the workflow map are contractually committed.
  • Co-marketing: OG images, UTM conventions, and post-event recap data cuts.

Data & analytics

  • Event naming schema across funnel, email, and landings; single guest id across steps.
  • Dashboards: funnel 0→9, drop at 4/4a–c, share virality (7), sponsor CTR (8).
  • Sampling vs full capture of policy link opens; GDPR-friendly retention for behavioral logs.

Engineering & integrations

  • ESP templates for Step 6: localization, dynamic modules, link tracking limits.
  • OAuth provider set (Google / Apple / Facebook) and fallback when provider is down.
  • Rate limits and CAPTCHA triggers on auth and share URL creation.
  • CDN cache rules for photopages and policy static pages.

Prep before the workshop

  • Current funnel numbers or a dry-run recording of the interactive journey on iOS + Android.
  • Print or PDF of this map + platform cards for annotating in the room.
  • List of open incidents (broken CTAs, consent bugs, email delays) to sanity-check against the map.
